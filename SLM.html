SLM Printed LatticeStructure

My research interest is in the application of computational design to additive manufacturing. During this time, I have used computational design methods to design lattice structures such as body-centred cubic, face-centred cubic, simple cubic and diamond-like lattices in different arrangements and have completed structural performance simulations and real-world compression tests on samples made of stainless steel with laser powder bed fusion process.
