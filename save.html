SAVE

This is a full-flow mobile app design project which aims to help implement cross-platform bookkeeping functionality, targeting the 18-27 year old demographic such as university students. The app is designed to address the pain point of contemporary young people who often use different payment methods and find it difficult to aggregate and organise them. Users can bundle some of their bank cards, mobile payment apps and other payment methods, enabling features such as bookkeeping, income and expenditure statistics and financial goals.
