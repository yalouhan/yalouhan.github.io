Rapid Forming Copper-Ammonia Fibre

Copper ammonia fibre is easily degraded and environmentally friendly. With its moisture absorption and breathable performance, the processed clothing can produce to avoid stuffiness and other uncomfortable feeling. Contact with the human body does not produce adverse reactions. At the same time, it has anti-insect and anti-corrosion characteristics, which is beneficial to health.
This project has revolutionised the production process for copper ammonia fibres by redesigning the copper ammonia fibre spinning liquor coagulation bath extrusion die, allowing it to produce a greater variety of shapes and developing the possibility of its parametric design. This process may have further possibilities for future applications in the medical and biological fields.
